ZKITS Zayedians Keep in Touch Society is basically a Zayedians Alumni platform, to keep the former and newer batches to the school and with one another. It is where the alumni will share their experiences with fresh Zayedian to help them through different matters. It is where all of the worthy students and teachers will get latest updates of what events are going on there and all the buzz of studies and entertainment. It will also help highlighting the achievements of the school and the students. I, Malik Hassan Raza, have developed this group as well as the website, both recognized and approved the administration and authorities of the school, for the first time in the 25 years history of the Sheikh Zayed Public School, RYK. All old and new worthy students as well as teachers are invited on this group to interact here. Thank you.
